### Global Merahan Programme – JNMS, Wuxi China

The GMP is an overseas immersion programme that aims to provide all Merahans with rich learning experiences beyond the classroom.

<img src="/images/wuxi1.png" style="width:80%">

The 2018 China GMP overseas immersion trip is led by our Vice-Principal, Ms Rebekah Tan. We will be visiting our partner school – Jiang Nan Middle School, Wuxi China. The GMP contingent comprises of teachers and students.
