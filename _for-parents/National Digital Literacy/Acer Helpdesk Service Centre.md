<h3>Acer Helpdesk Service Centre</h3>
<p>Students who encountered any hardware or software issues with their PLDs
may contact the Acer helpdesk.</p>
<p>For any service case, please&nbsp;<strong>first</strong>&nbsp;write to
the helpdesk email with the following:</p>
<p><strong>Subject header:</strong>&nbsp;MOE PLD</p>
<p><strong>Content:</strong>&nbsp;School, Device model, Serial number, Brief
description of problem, Person to contact and Contact number</p>
