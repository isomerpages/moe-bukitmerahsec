<h4><strong>PREPARING OUR STUDENTS FOR THE FUTURE EMERGING 21ST CENTURY COMPETENCIES (E21CC) @ BMSS:</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<h4><strong><u>Overview of E21CC:</u></strong></h4>
<p>At Bukit Merah Secondary School (BMSS), we are committed to nurturing
Emerging 21st Century Competencies (E21CC) in our students. These competencies
equip Merahans with the skills, values, and dispositions needed to navigate
the challenges of the future. Building upon the foundation of five social-emotional
competencies (Self Awareness, Self Management, Social Awareness, Relationship
Management, and Responsible Decision-Making), our school is committed to
fostering students to be confident, empathetic, and motivated lifelong
learners.</p>
<p>The E21CC are categorized into three key domains, with each domain further
detailed to identify the specific observable and actionable skills and
dispositions that we aim for our students to demonstrate within the BMSS
context.</p>

<h4><strong><u>Learning Opportunities for E21CC Development:</u></strong></h4>
<p>BMSS adopts a holistic approach to E21CC development through our total
curriculum. Students at BMSS engage in various platforms to develop E21CC,
including:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Core Curriculum</strong>:
E21CC development is integrated into our core academic subjects, and Character
and Citizenship Education programmes.. This ensures that competency development
occurs naturally within the context of subject mastery.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Co-Curriculum</strong>: Our
extensive range of co-curricular activities, leadership development programmes
and Values-in-Action projects provide practical platforms for students
to apply and refine their 21st century competencies in real-world contexts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Signature Student Development Experiences</strong>:
Distinctive school events such as GROW Week, the Merahan Olympics, and
our Global Merahan Programme create unique opportunities for students to
demonstrate and develop their competencies through immersive, collaborative
experiences.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Strategic Partnerships:</strong> We
actively collaborate with community stakeholders, industry partners, and
educational institutions to enhance our E21CC initiatives and provide students
with authentic learning opportunities beyond the classroom.</p>
</li>
</ul>
